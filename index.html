Job Description

As a DevOps Engineer, we empower our agile cross-functional teams as experts to ensure reliability, maximum automation and performance of applications through rapid and continuous development. Our team is spreaded across Belgium, France and Germany.

WE ARE LOOKING FOR AN INDIVIDUAL WHO CAN:

  • Practice an Agile mindset (team player)

  • Coach the DevOps culture in cross-discipline and cross-functional product teams.

  • Ensure maximum automation by DevOps tools like IaC tools

  • You are enthusiastic about modern technologies and knowledge sharing (e.g. pair programming)

  • You appreciate an environment where feedback culture is important

  • Ensure the usage of CI/CD pipelines

  • Ensure monitoring/alerting of used infrastructure/application components

Qualifications

AN INDIVIDUAL WHO HAS:

  • General experience in "Infrastructure as Code" tools like AWS CDK, Terraform

  • Experience in the usage of the Cloud (AWS preferred, or with GCP, Azure

  • General Experience in CI/CD tools like CircleCI, Github Actions, CircleCI, GitlabCI

  •  General experience in Alerting/Monitoring of infrastructure components (at best Datadog)

  • Experience in version control like Git and their systems like GitHub, GitLab
